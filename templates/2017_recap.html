Top 25 presidential tweets for 2017

This is a nonpartisan list of the tweets from President Donald Trump that got the highest engagement during 2017. The ranking is a simple one, adding together "retweets" and "likes." My own theory is that "likes" most often show support, while "retweets" simply represent sharing the tweet with others and cannot be interpreted as approval or disapproval.

While Trump took over the @POTUS account on Jan. 20, 2017, he primarily uses his longstanding personal account, @realDonaldTrump. (To see his @POTUS tweets, click here.)
