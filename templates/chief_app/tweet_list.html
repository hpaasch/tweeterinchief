Top 20 Most Popular presidential tweets

This is a nonpartisan constantly updating list of the Top 20 Most Popular tweets from President Donald Trump. The "most popular" calculation is a simple one, adding together "retweets" and "likes." My own theory is that "likes" most often show support, while "retweets" simply represent sharing the tweet with others and cannot be interpreted as approval or disapproval.

The first list is the @POTUS account Trump took over on Jan. 20, 2017. The second list is Trump's longstanding @realDonaldTrump account, but looking only at tweets made as of January 2017. Both lists update each time you return, so check back often!
